# Welcome to Folk Lab

Welcome to our new space dedicated to exploring the intersection of traditional folk practices and modern innovation. This is where we document our journey, share insights, and build community.

## What We're About

Folk Lab is a collaborative space for:

- Exploring traditional folk practices through a contemporary lens
- Documenting cultural heritage and storytelling
- Building connections between past and present
- Experimenting with new forms of folk expression

## Join Us

We're excited to have you here. Stay tuned for regular updates, stories, and insights from our community.
